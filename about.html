About Us

The Driver and Vehicle Licensing Authority(DVLA) is a public-sector organisation under the Ministry of Transport. It was established in 1999 by an Act of Parliament,1999. It is responsible for Issuing Driving Licence,Issuing vehicle registration certificates,Maintain registers containing particulars of licence motor vehicles.
